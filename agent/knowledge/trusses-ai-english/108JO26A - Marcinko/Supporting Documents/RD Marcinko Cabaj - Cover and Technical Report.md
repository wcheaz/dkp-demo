4.1. EARTHWORKS 
   Before starting excavation works, rough terrain adjustments (RTA) must be carried out,
consisting of removing the topsoil layer and any original fills. The elevation level
for RTA as well as individual subgrade adjustments are the subject of the project for realization -
building part of the project documentation for the placement of the building based on the actual
elevation survey and terrain plan. The frost-free depth of the foundation trench (below the soil
horizon layer) will be created by terrain adjustments or by adjusting the elevation grade line around the building
and the surrounding terrain according to the construction supervisor's instructions - in shallower
horizons of the foundation trench, protection against frost heave will be achieved by backfilling
foundation structures with soil fill placed on natural terrain.
    In the building placement area, I strongly point out the possible occurrence of fills,
high-plasticity fine-grained (cohesive) soils, loose to liquid sands / gravels in the
subsoil, whose consistency state, compactness and other physical-mechanical
characteristics are influenced by moisture, groundwater, and which may be of low
bearing capacity, easily compressible, volume-unstable, sensitive to water, therefore during excavation works
```
I recommend summoning the responsible geologist and structural engineer to take over or to evaluate
the foundation trench, to verify the actual soils in the subsoil and to determine the bearing capacity
of the foundation soil, who will determine and confirm by record the actual properties of the soil in the foundation
trench. Upon uncovering the foundation trench, I request verification of the conformity of the actual soils in the foundation
trench with the assumptions. In case of discrepancies between the foundation soil and the assumptions, it is necessary to
```
re-evaluate the foundation structures!
    Excavation works are recommended to be carried out mechanically (the last 100-150mm to be excavated
manually) and in dry periods - to minimize the risk of deterioration of the foundation trench by various
climatic and mechanical influences. Immediately before concreting the foundations, cleaning of
the excavation bottom is necessary. During construction, the foundation trench must be protected against

or alternatively from Heluz system lintels. The ceiling is designed as a timber ceiling on load-bearing
walls and load-bearing lintels. The roof is designed as a hip roof with concrete or
ceramic tiles.
