Marginal notes
1.  STRUCTURAL LOAD
LOAD:
- PERMANENT ROOF LOAD:850 N/m² (650N/m2 - roof, 200N/m2 - PV panels)
- PERMANENT CEILING LOAD:450 N/m²
- SNOW LOAD:1050 N/m²
- WIND LOAD:627 N/m²
2.  TRUSS SUPPORT
ALL WALLS AND BEAMS THAT ARE CONSIDERED AS TRUSS SUPPORTS ARE
MARKED IN THE FLOOR PLAN. OTHER WALLS AND PARTITIONS MUST BE LOWER BY MIN.
50mm.
3.  STORAGE SPACE
PART OF THE ROOF SPACE IS DESIGNED AS STORAGE SPACE (hatched area in
floor plan). THE SPACE IS INTENDED FOR STORING LIGHTWEIGHT ITEMS.
Permanent load (Floor): 250N/m2
Live load: 750N/m2
