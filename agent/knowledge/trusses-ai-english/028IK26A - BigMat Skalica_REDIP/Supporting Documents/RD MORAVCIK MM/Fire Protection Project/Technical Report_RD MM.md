FIRE SAFETY SOLUTION OF THE BUILDING
Introduction
 
The prepared building project for building permit addresses the new construction of a "Family house" in the cadastral area of Jakubov.  
According to Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z. z., the building is classified as a building of group A with at most two residential units and a building
of a family house.
 
Description of the building
 
The building is designed as single-storey. The entrance to the building is via the 1st floor of the building, width 900 mm /1.5 escape lanes/.  
Layout design see Drawing section.  
Fire height of the building is hp = 0 m.
 
Structural design of the building
 
The proposed building is designed from a combustible structural assembly.
The building is founded on foundation strips made of plain concrete and the perimeter masonry is designed from AAC blocks thickness
300 mm, with thermal insulation made of EPS thickness 150 mm. Internal load-bearing walls are designed from AAC blocks thickness 250 mm,
non-load-bearing partitions thickness 125 mm.   
Surface finishes inside the building are of fire reaction class A1 according to STN EN 13 501-1. External surface finishes –
exterior plasters and cladding are of fire reaction class A1 according to STN EN 13 501-1. The roof structure is formed by timber
framing. Floors are ceramic tiles and laminate floors.
 
*STN 92 0201-2 Building products and their components that are added to structural elements, forming their surface finish. 
Surface finish is not taken into account when classifying the structural element.
 
Division of building into fire sections
 
The building is divided into the following fire sections in accordance with Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z.Z. :
N1.01 – Family house
 
Fire safety
 
The calculated fire load for fire section N1.01 is according to STN 92 0201-1, Annex K, Table K.1, item 16:
pv = 50 kg.m-2 and coefficient "a" = 1.0.
 
Size of fire sections
 
The permitted area of the fire section is not determined if it is a fire section without fire risk and a fire section
of a protected escape route and if the floor plan area of the fire section is at most 300 m2.
In reality, fire section N1.01 has an area of max. S = 85.08 m2.
 
Degree of fire safety
 
According to STN 92 0201-2/O1 Art. 3.4, buildings intended for housing of group A are classified into degree I of fire safety.

The fire wall must adjoin the fire ceiling or must extend above the roof covering by 450 mm. No fire closure is proposed
in the building, the building forms one fire section.   
The proposed family house has a roof with the function of a fire ceiling.  
 
The load-bearing structures of the roof are protected by fire-resistant plasterboard thickness 12.5 mm.  
Visible wooden elements in the space must meet the load-bearing requirement R with a resistance of at least 15 min. Load-bearing wooden
structures that are visible must be protected by fire-resistant coating or cladding, spraying, or masonry encasement.
 
According to Decree 94/2004 §44 para. 7 c, the provision of fire breaks is not required.
 
Escape routes
 
Escape of persons from the building in question is ensured via exits from the building to open space.  
In accordance with § 94 para. 7 of Decree No. 94/2004 Z.z. as amended, the escape route in the building must be at least 0.9 m wide and the width
of doors on this escape route must not be less than 0.8 m. (actual execution: leaf of main entrance doors – 900
mm).

It is not proposed in the building in accordance with Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z. z.

Separation distances do not encroach on neighboring buildings. Separation distances are indicated in the drawing section.  
Pipeline distributions and technical and technological equipment used for the transport of combustible substances located in the fire
hazardous space must comply with Art. 2.7.2 STN 92 0201-4.
 
Water for fire extinguishing
 
Internal hose equipment is not proposed in the building in accordance with STN 92 0400 and Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 699/2004 Z. z. /§10,
paragraph 2a/.  
 
To ensure water for fire extinguishing according to STN 92 400 Table 2, a water supply pipe of dimension DN80 Q = 7.5 l.s with
a fire pump is required.
The position of the external hydrant must be a minimum of 5 m and a maximum of 200 m from the building. The hydrant must not be located in the fire
hazardous space.  
The fire water requirement will be ensured by means of a hydrant, which is located at a distance of up to 25 m from the building.
 
Note: The obligations of municipalities, legal entities, natural persons-entrepreneurs and natural persons in the area of fire protection are regulated by Act No.
314/2001 Z. z. second part.
 
Securing of fire sections
 
For the fire section, according to Decree 94/2004, §87, 88, 90, a fixed fire extinguishing system (SHZ),
electrical fire alarm system (EPS) or smoke and combustion products exhaust system (ZODSH) are not required for the given fire section.
Voice fire alarm is not required by Decree 94/2004.
Emergency lighting is not required according to Decree 94/2004, §73.  
Marking of ESCAPE ROUTE is not required.
 
Portable fire extinguishers:
 
They meet the requirements of STN 92 0202-1
 
Required amount of extinguishing agent 1st floor Mc = 8.5 kg  
Proposed amount of extinguishing agent Mc = 12 kg
 
Type of fire extinguisher: 2x powder 6 kg
 
I recommend equipping the fire section with portable fire extinguishers according to STN 92 0202, the position is marked in the drawing
section.  
Conditions for installation and operation of portable fire extinguishers:  
The type of portable fire extinguisher must be designed with regard to combustible substances in the building and the extinguishing efficiency of fire extinguishers. In accordance with
STN 92 0202-1, the proposed portable fire extinguisher must be placed in a permanently accessible and clearly visible location (generally on vertical
building structures or on the floor according to the manufacturer's instructions). The portable fire extinguisher must be placed at an appropriate height depending on
its weight and so that the handle of the extinguisher is at most 1.5 meters above the floor level, and it must be protected from
direct effects of solar radiation and adverse effects of the environment. The portable fire extinguisher station must be visibly marked
with a pictogram in accordance with Art. 7.1.4 STN 92 0202-1, it is marked with a pictogram according to the SR Government Regulation No. 387/2006. If
the access route to the portable fire extinguisher station is not clearly visible, the pictogram marking the portable fire extinguisher station must be supplemented with an additional
pictogram of fire protection signs indicating the direction. The placement of portable fire extinguishers must not obstruct the evacuation of persons from the building
endangered by fire or otherwise impede it.  
All requirements of Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 719/2002 Z.z. must be met.
Note : The number and type of portable fire extinguishers deviating from the proposal in this fire safety solution may be adjusted and adapted to operational needs,
however, the total required equivalent amount of extinguishing agent (Mc) for the given fire section must be maintained (see fire safety calculations).

Ventilation in the building is natural.

Heating
 
Heating of the building is by means of a heat pump.
For detailed heating design see the heating section.
When installing heat appliances and heat sources and a fireplace, the requirements of Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 401/2007 Z.
z. will be taken into account.
 
Access routes
 
Access areas and intervention routes are not proposed in the building in accordance with §83 of Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z. z.  
Internal intervention routes do not need to be constructed according to §84, para. 1a, as the length and width of the building is less than 30 m.  
External intervention routes – ladder are not required according to §86, as the floor plan area is less than 200 m2.
 
The access road must not be more than 50 m from the building according to §82 of Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z. z.  
The building will be accessed by a road that will be approximately 9 m from the building.
 
This road must meet according to § 82 para. 3 - The access road must have a permanently clear width of at least 3 m and its
load-bearing capacity for loading by a single vehicle axle must be at least 80 kN; the parking lane
is not included in the permanently clear width. Every non-driveable single-lane access road longer than 50 m must have a turning loop at the end or
an area enabling the turning of a vehicle.
 
Requirements for electrical, cable distributions and installations

Central disconnection of electrical appliances of the house will be possible via the main switch of distribution board RH. Disconnection of the building from the electrical
network will be possible via the main circuit breaker of the meter distribution board of the house RE. Requirements for cables according to standard STN 92 0203
– Annex B2.4 – there are none.

Conclusion

The prepared building project for building permit takes into account the requirements of Decree of the Ministry of Interior of the SR No. 94/2004 Z. z., as the main
and other related standards and regulations in the field of fire safety of buildings.
