2.  TRUSS SUPPORT
ALL WALLS AND BEAMS THAT ARE CONSIDERED AS TRUSS SUPPORTS ARE
MARKED IN THE FLOOR PLAN. OTHER WALLS AND PARTITIONS MUST BE LOWER MIN. BY
50mm.

the floor plan). THE SPACE IS USED FOR STORING LIGHTWEIGHT OBJECTS.
Permanent load (FLOOR): 250N/m2
Live load: 750N/m2
