1. STRUCTURAL AND TECHNICAL SOLUTION OF THE BUILDING 
 
1.1. Earthworks 
According to the conditions determined in the building permit, before starting earthworks 
vytýči poloha navrhovaného of a family house. Takisto sa zreteľne označí výškový bod, od ktorého 
all relevant heights are determined shall also be clearly marked, ±0,000 = 206,2 m a.s.l. (Bpv, DMR5). The actual earthworks will 
begin with clearing the surface of the original layer. The depth of removed topsoil is 0,200m. The 
excavation works themselves will be carried out mechanically with manual trimming of the foundation trench. The foundation 
trench is at the level of -1,380m + gravel bed -1,530m. The excavation pit is proposed at the level of -0,430 
m. 
The stabilized groundwater level is below the level of the foundation trenches, as well as below the level of 
sewer channels in the foundations. The excavated soil will be kept on the construction site for backfill and adjustment of 
the surrounding terrain. Subsequently, mechanical and manual trench excavation will be carried out for HVAC ducts, horizontal 
sewerage and for the building sewer system and building water supply. After backfilling 
the sewer pipes, it is necessary to stabilize the trench backfill to the strength of the original terrain. Excavations 
sa vymerajú a prevedú podľa stavebných výkresov niveláciou. Viď. Projekt stavebná časť- 
floor plan of foundations. If unsuitable foundation conditions are proven, it is necessary to 
reassess the method of building foundation!!! 
1.2. Foundations 
   An engineering-geological survey was carried out at the proposed construction site, for the needs of 
the building permit project documentation, findings from the given survey in the 
given locality of Kúcanka.  
Foundations objektu bude realizované na základových pásoch zo železobetónu hrúbky 
600 mm mm, under internal load-bearing walls the strips are w. 900 mm and height 500 mm and on foundation 
pad (dimensions will be assessed directly on site after excavation of foundation trenches and evaluation of 
the foundation soil in the presumed sufficiently load-bearing layer of weathered and disintegrated 
crystalline rocks). In case of permeable subsoil layers, a sub-bed will be created under the 
foundation strips from gravel of thickness 150 mm. 
Medzi základovými pásmi a základovou doskou sú navrhované steny z debniacich 
blocks of thickness 300 mm are proposed, alternatively from concrete masonry units. Type and procedure will be determined 
by the contractor based on subsoil during excavation works. Penetrations of horizontal utility network distribution 
are implemented through the foundation wall. 
The foundation slab of thickness 150 mm from concrete needs to be reinforced with mesh reinforcement B 500A 
Φ8/8 mm, openings 150/150 mm with overlap of two openings at splicing. The mesh needs to 
be placed in 1/3 of the subbase concrete thickness from the bottom surface. The subgrade under the foundation 
slab shall be adjusted with compacted gravel bed (material - sub-bed base: classified gravel of fraction 8-
32 mm) hr. 150 mm (Triedu zhutnenia urči realizátor pri výkopoch podľa vlastností podložia) 
separated from the foundation slab by geotextile (min. 200kg/m2) 
Preliminary dimensions of foundation structures and foundation depth are evident from the structural  
part of the project documentation. 
It is necessary that foundation structures reach the frost-free depth! 
 
1.3. Structural solution 
The building consists of one expansion joint unit. The masonry is proposed from piece masonry 
on adhesive mortar, thin-layer adhesive or PUR foam (according to the technological procedure). The building 
has 2 above-ground floors and no basement.

Ceiling structure 
Above the 1st floor, a monolithic reinforced concrete ceiling Th.250mm is proposed. In this ceiling structure, HVAC

Chimney 
Exhaust of combustion gases from the stove will be solved through a three-layer stainless steel chimney Ø 200mm (0,6mm) 
insulation 30mm, routed through the exterior along the facade. 
• 
Staircase 
It is proposed as a single-flight right-turning staircase from monolithic concrete, built into the reinforced concrete ceiling 
• 
Lintels above openings 
In external walls, ceramic load-bearing lintels are used above openings up to an opening width of 2 
meters. Above openings with a width greater than 2 meters, reinforced concrete lintels are used unless 
otherwise specified by the structural engineer. 
In internal walls above door openings, ceramic load-bearing or non-load-bearing 
lintels are used.   
 
1.4. Material solution 
   It is based on the use of proven natural materials applying their 
natural structure and color. 
    
• 
Floor structures 
Floors in the building are proposed with respect to the requirements for their solution according to the purpose 
of the rooms. Wearing layers are designed as tiles or laminate parquet. Insulation of floors 
from external walls is ensured by inserting expansion joint strips.  
Wearing layers of floors in individual rooms are as follows: 
- WC, technical room, vestibule - tiles (color and structure of tiles to be determined by the 
investor before implementation) 
- hallway, rooms, common room, bedroom - per investor's choice 
Part of the bathroom floor composition is a layer of waterproofing coating, which is 
continuously applied also to moisture-loaded wall surfaces. The junction of wall and floor coating must be 
reinforced in the corner. 
Interfaces between individual floor wearing layers will be finished with expansion 
joint strips.  
Part of the floor wearing layers are perimeter skirting boards, which cover the expansion joint strip 
of the floating floor at the junction with vertical structures.  
All floor structure compositions are documented in detail in the section drawing 
• 
Roof structures 
The roof framing structure is designed as a wooden truss structure supplied 
by the supplier. 
The roof slope is 3° formed by the truss structure.

2. ECONOMIC EVALUATION OF THE BUILDING 
The building is, for its character, without complex shapes, details and structural solutions. 
The building structure itself therefore creates a precondition for a financially advantageous investment while 
maintaining the established standard. In the interior, we propose using economically standard 
surface treatments and furnishings appropriate for a family house. 
3. ENVIRONMENTAL CARE 
Realizácia of a family house nie je v rozpore s platnou územnoplánovacou dokumentáciou mesta 
Studienka. 
Placement of the building is on a plot without claim to agricultural land. 
The given construction will not have a negative impact on the environment. It has a non-production character. 
In accordance with §8 of the Building Act, it will not have negative effects and impacts, will not produce 
harmful emissions, noise, heat, vibrations, vibrations, dust, odor, glare and shading, and will not 
deteriorate the environment on the building site and its surroundings beyond the permissible level.  
During construction and during subsequent operation, it is not necessary to establish even temporary 
protective hygiene zones. The prepared project documentation respects Act No. 24/2006 
the Act on Environmental Impact Assessment and on amendment and supplementation of certain laws.  
Given the location of the construction site, we propose building materials to be delivered 
to the construction site palletized and stored. 
Water protection will be ensured by several technical measures (water-management 
secured areas, drainage of wastewater into public sewer).. 
The overall technical solution, designed parameters are proposed with awareness of minimizing 
the impact on the environment, while all valid legislative regulations are taken into account. 
All necessary infrastructure for the proposed activity is available in the locality. 
Implementation of the activity will not lead to exceeding environmental quality standards. 
Potentially adverse impacts can be eliminated by preventive protective measures. 
From the perspective of possible risk, the potentially adverse impact is considered to be the potential threat to the quality 
of air, groundwater and surface water in cases of unforeseen events (technological equipment failure 
or leakage of hazardous substances into an unsecured environment, etc.).. 
Prevention, averting, elimination and disposal of possible consequences of accidents will be 
the subject of solutions and measures in further stages of project preparation of the proposed activity ako 
aj havarijných a prevádzkových plánov. 
Given the nature and scope of the activity, the place of performing the activity, the bearable load  
and the significance of expected impacts, it can be stated that in the proposed locality, implementation of 
the activity in connection with the surroundings will not lead to exceeding environmental quality standards. 
The expected impacts do not reach the level of significant impacts on individual components of the 
environment and can be mitigated by suitable technical or organizational measures.

Technological procedure during which waste arises and procedure for its disposal: 
In connection with construction, construction wastes will arise during excavation works 
and the actual implementation of the building.  
Wastes will be collected separately in containers by type so that suitable 
wastes can be recycled. All wastes by individual types will be recorded. 
Excavated soil will be kept on the construction site for backfill and adjustment of the surrounding terrain. 
 
Excess soil will be used for backfill and adjustment of the surrounding terrain. 
Excavation soil will be checked for the presence of hazardous substances; if such 
substances are identified, the contaminated excavated soils will be handled as 
hazardous waste in accordance with Act No. 79/2015 Coll. on waste. 
After completion of construction, the contractor, in cooperation with the building investor, shall submit to the department 
of the relevant state administration body, for each completion proceedings, a record of construction wastes from 
stavieb a doklady o ich zneškodnení, zmluvu na odvoz a zneškodňovanie komunálneho of waste. 
During waste management, the building contractor will also respect the conditions contained in 
Act No. 79/2015 Coll. On waste and related regulations.
